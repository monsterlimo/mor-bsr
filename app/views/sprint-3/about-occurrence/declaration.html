If you are an accountable person (AP), principal accountable person (PAP), principal client, contractor or designer, you must make a MOR for a building in the event of a safety occurence which risks structural failure or spread of fire.

You commit a criminal offence if you do not make a MOR within 10 days of becoming aware of an occurrence.

A safety occurence is defined as an event which risks structural failure or fire spreading in the building.

What happens next

The building Safety Regulator (BSR) will raise your complaint with the relevant accountable persons or organisations.

We will not share your name or details if you told us you wanted to remain anonymous when you submitted your complaint.

If we need more information from you during our investigation, we will email you.

We will email you with the outcome of our investigation and tell you what, if any, enforcement action we are going to take.

Investigations take time. If you don’t hear from us after nn working days, contact us, quoting your reference number.

Email or phone BSR on 0300 790 6787 if you don’t hear from us after 21 working days, contact BSR, quoting your reference number.

BSR is open Monday to Friday from 8.30am to 5pm, except Wednesdays when we are open from 10am to 5pm.
